Welcome to Hot Beans!


At Hot Beans, we’re passionate about crafting exceptional digital experiences that help businesses succeed online. We’re not just web developers—we’re problem-solvers, designers, and strategists dedicated to building websites and applications that blend creativity with functionality.


We believe in delivering high-quality web solutions that are not only visually stunning but also fast, secure, and user-friendly. Whether it’s a sleek business website, a powerful e-commerce platform, or a bespoke web application, we ensure every project is designed to make an impact.


But it’s not just about writing code. We take a holistic approach, considering strategy, design, performance, and scalability to create solutions that grow with your business.


Our values

At Hot Beans, our core principles shape everything we do—from the way we work with clients to the quality of the websites we build. We believe that great web development goes beyond just writing code; it’s about creating digital experiences that are engaging, user-friendly, and built to last. Our approach is rooted in collaboration, innovation, and a commitment to delivering results that make a real difference for businesses.


We take the time to understand our clients’ needs, ensuring that every project we undertake is tailored to their goals and audience. By combining technical expertise with creative problem-solving, we develop solutions that not only look great but also perform seamlessly across all devices.


Our values guide us in every step of the development process, helping us create websites and applications that are as functional as they are visually appealing. These values include:

  • Quality – Writing clean, efficient, and high-performing code that ensures reliability and security.
  • User-Centred Design – Crafting intuitive and accessible digital experiences that put users first.
  • Innovation – Staying ahead of the latest web technologies, tools, and trends to deliver cutting-edge solutions.
  • Collaboration – Building strong partnerships with our clients, working closely to bring their vision to life.
  • Reliability – Meeting deadlines, exceeding expectations, and providing ongoing support to ensure long-term success.
  • Scalability – Creating future-proof digital solutions that can grow and adapt alongside businesses.

Our Journey

Hot Beans was founded out of a passion for technology and a drive to help businesses establish a strong online presence. Our journey began when our founders, Alex and Jamie, recognised the need for beautifully designed, high-performing websites that balance creativity with technical excellence.


What started as a small freelance venture quickly grew into a thriving digital agency known for producing outstanding web solutions. Over the years, we’ve helped businesses of all sizes—from ambitious start-ups to established enterprises—develop engaging websites and innovative online platforms.


Today, Hot Beans continues to evolve, but we remain true to our roots. We’re proud to offer a place where every cup of coffee tells a story, and every visit feels like coming home. From the first sip to the last bite, we invite you to be part of our journey and share in the joy of good coffee with us.


Proudly serving our community and recognised as one of the city’s favourite coffee spots, we are grateful to all our customers who have made this journey possible.
