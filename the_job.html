Key Requirements


  • A strong understanding of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to build and style web pages effectively. Experience with front-end frameworks such as React, Vue.js, or Angular to create interactive and dynamic user experiences.

  • Ability to ensure website security, prevent vulnerabilities, and maintain site integrity. Experience in performance optimisation to improve loading speeds and overall user experience. Knowledge of SEO best practices to enhance website visibility, alongside strong debugging and troubleshooting skills to resolve issues quickly.
  • Experience in e-commerce platforms or online ordering systems to improve customer experience. Understanding of UX/UI design principles to create visually appealing and user-friendly interfaces. Familiarity with cloud hosting services such as AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud for website deployment. Basic graphic design skills in tools like Figma or Adobe XD for layout and design adjustments.
